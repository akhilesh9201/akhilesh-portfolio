About

I'm a UX Designer with 3 years of experience across B2B, B2C, associate, and enterprise products, mostly at Walmart scale. I gravitate toward hard workflow problems: the kind where multiple teams are involved, nothing quite talks to each other, and the user ends up stuck in the middle.

I've worked across a pretty wide range: consumer-facing journeys, procurement and negotiation tools, mobile workflows for frontline associates, and large enterprise systems that sourcing teams live in every day. The common thread is making complex systems feel less painful for the people inside them.

I've also been using AI to move faster and think sharper in my own process. Where I'm headed is designing AI products that feel genuinely useful to everyday people, not just impressive in a demo.
